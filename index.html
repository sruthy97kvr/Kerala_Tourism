Tour Of Thekkady


Thekkady is a town near Periyar National Park, an
important tourist attraction in the Kerala state of India.
The name Thekkady is derived from the word "thekku" which means teak.
Temperatures are lowest in the months of December–January and
highest in the months of April–May.
